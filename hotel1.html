Club Waterford












Star Rating



Treacy's Hotel and Leisure Club Waterford is situated in Waterford City. Overlooking the River Suir. Guests can dine in Croker's Restaurant, enjoy a drink in Timbertoes bar, relax at the Spirit Leisure Centre.


Treacy's Hotel and Leisure Club Waterford is a quality three star heritage hotel that combines modern comfort with quintessential Irish style, featuring spectacular Waterford Crystal chandeliers and rich oak paneling. This family-run hotel is situated in the heart of Waterford City in Ireland's Sunny South East. Treacy's Hotel and Leisure Club Waterford combines old world charm and traditional hospitality with modern amenities. They offer excellent guest bedrooms with 130 refurbished bedrooms, modern conferencing facilities, delicious food and top quality service.

Star Rating

The Kilford Arms Hotel is enviably located, just yards from the train and bus station and within walking distance to all major sights and attractions that Kilkenny City has to offer.


The Kilford Arms Hotel stands in Kilkenny City Centre. With 60 bedrooms, it is small enough to offer a truly personal service, while offering an excellent range of facilities. The ensuite guest bedrooms at the Kilford Arms Hotel are equipped to high standards with satellite TV, telephones, hairdryers, trouser press and, tea and coffee making facilities.
